Types of Cyber Attacks:

  • Phishing: Fake emails or messages tricking you into revealing personal info.
  • Malware: Harmful software that damages your system or steals data.
  • Ransomware: Locks your files until a ransom is paid.
  • Identity Theft: Stealing your personal data to impersonate you.
  • Social Engineering:Manipulating people into giving up confidential information.

Social Media Safety Tips:

  • Use strong, unique passwords for each account.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
  • Be cautious about sharing personal information.
  • Review and update privacy settings regularly.
  • Think before clicking on unknown links.
  • Do not share personal information publicly.

Safe Practices & Unsafe Actions:

Safe Practices:

  • Keep software and apps updated.
  • Report suspicious activities.
  • Log out from shared devices.
  • Use trusted and secure networks.

Unsafe Actions:

  • Accepting friend requests from strangers.
  • Sharing passwords with others.
  • Clicking on suspicious ads or links.
  • Oversharing personal information online.
